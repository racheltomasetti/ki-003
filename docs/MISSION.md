# MISSION.md

## What Ki Is

Ki is a Self awareness tool. A context builder. A thinking partner for anyone in pursuit of the life they desire.

It is not a note-taking app. It is not a journal. It is not a second brain or a productivity system. It is the space where your raw, unfinished thinking becomes something you can actually work with — where captures accumulate into clarity, and clarity becomes action.

The name comes from the concept of life force — the energy within all of us that makes us feel most alive. Ki is built around the belief that when you are living in alignment with what drives you, inspiration is everywhere. A conversation, a billboard, a quote, a moment of stillness — all of it is revealing something about what you are already working through. Ki catches it, holds it, and helps you think with it over time.

Ki is built for those in pursuit.

## The Philosophical Core

Building your Self and building systems are the same act.

Same abstraction. Same logic. Same principle: garbage in, garbage out. What you feed your mind shapes who you become. What you feed a system shapes what it produces. Ki is built on this parallel — not as metaphor, but as methodology.

Your thoughts are the raw material. Unfinished, tangled, half-formed — the hay. Ki is the wheel that spins it into gold. The quality of what emerges depends on the intentionality of what you bring.

## The Central Entities

Ki is organized around a few core entities. How these relate to each other is still being discovered through use. What follows is the current understanding — subject to evolution.

The Pursuit 
A pursuit is something you are carrying. A question you are living, a thing you are building, a direction you are moving in. At its center is a core question — the guiding light that makes new information relevant or not. Pursuits are active antennas. They are not organizational containers. Captures do not get filed into pursuits — they connect to pursuits through resonance, automatically, based on what they are about.

A pursuit deepens over time. It does not close. It evolves as you do.

Curiosities 
The broader landscape of what you are sitting with — questions that have not yet demanded full focus, directions you might move, things percolating beneath the surface. A curiosity can crystallize into a pursuit when it becomes alive enough to carry a core question.

Projects 
A project is a specific thing you are building toward a real outcome. It has edges — a shape, an end state, a sequence of decisions and actions that lead somewhere concrete. Projects emerge from pursuits or stand alone. They are thinking spaces where you work something all the way through mentally before taking the first physical step. The project is not a task list. It is the runway that makes action possible.

## The Ki Document

Every agent conversation in Ki is primed with a living document before it begins — the user's Ki document. This is the context layer that makes Ki feel like it already knows you.

It holds who you are and how you think, what you are building or becoming, what you are carrying right now, what you have tried and found lacking, and what you want from Ki.

The Ki document is never finished. It is built through onboarding, updated through conversations and captures, and versioned over time. Every update is layered, not replaced. The document becomes a longitudinal record of the user's evolution — who they were when they started, what shifted, how their thinking changed. The path from unclear to clarity, documented in real time.

---

## The Corpus

Everything a user captures lives in one unified pool. No silos. No manual routing.

The enrichment pipeline does the organizing — extracting themes, entities, sentiment, and connecting each capture to the pursuits it resonates with and why. The user does not file captures. The system makes the connections. The user explores, filters, and directs.

The corpus is not static. When a new pursuit is added, Ki looks back across everything previously captured and tests it against the new pursuit and its core question. The past reorganizes around the present.

What becomes possible as the corpus deepens is still being discovered. That is part of what we are building toward.

---

## The Flow

Capture anywhere. Think deeply on the web.

Capture is available on both mobile and web. Mobile exists for convenience — inspiration does not wait, and life happens away from a screen. The web is the primary thinking environment: the oasis where you return to do the deeper work of distilling, connecting, and arriving at clarity.

1. You carry live pursuits — questions and intentions alive for you right now 
2. You move through the world 
3. Something resonates — you capture it: voice, text, image, observation 
4. Ki holds it, enriches it, connects it to what you are already carrying 
5. The corpus deepens over time 
6. Ki surfaces what is emerging — patterns, tensions, clarity 
7. You act, create, decide, become 
8. New captures follow. The cycle continues.

## Who This Is For

Ki is for anyone intentionally creating something in their life — externally or internally. A business, a body of work, a mindset, an identity, a life. The builder and the person becoming are the same person.

The specific human Ki is built for: someone who knows they are in a growth period. Who feels the weight of their own thinking. Who has tried other tools and found them lacking — because those tools store but do not think back. Who is drowning in their own depth and needs something that meets them there.

Ki is built for those in pursuit. The person who reads that and feels it — that is the user.

---

## What Ki Is Not

Ki does not replace thinking — it extends it. Ki does not demand daily use — it rewards return. Ki does not measure progress — progress here is internal, felt as clarity and alignment, known only by the person living it. Ki is not an obligation. It is an oasis. Your sanctuary.
